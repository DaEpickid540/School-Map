Before You Start

This works on any Android browser — Chrome, Firefox, Edge, or any other. Make sure you're connected to Wi-Fi when you first install it.

Step-by-step instructions
1
Open your browser & navigate to the kiosk
Open Chrome, Firefox, or your preferred browser on your Android phone and go to the Mason Navigator URL. You can get this by scanning the QR code shown on the kiosk display at school, or by typing the address directly.

Tap the menu button
At the top right of your browser, tap the three-dot menu button (⋮). Look for an option that says "Install app", "Add to Home screen", or "Create shortcut".

3
Select "Install app" or "Add to Home Screen"
Tap the install option. A dialog will appear asking you to confirm adding Mason Navigator to your home screen.

Find the icon on your Home Screen
Mason Navigator will appear as an icon on your Android home screen — just like a regular app. Press the home button to go back and find it.

Open it — it works like a real app!
Tap the icon to launch Mason Navigator. It opens full screen, with no browser address bar or buttons — just the app. Once loaded, it works even without a network connection.

Route links from the kiosk's QR code expire after 30 minutes — so scan and open them right away. The installed app itself never expires.

You're all set!

Mason Navigator is now on your Home Screen. Scan any QR code from a kiosk to instantly load your route.
